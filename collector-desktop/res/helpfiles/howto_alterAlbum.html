How-to alter an existing Album

If you want to alter an existing album, then simply use the "Alter selected Album" entry within the Album menu item

This will then show all available update possibilities within the right sidebar. While the renaming of an album is executed by clicking the rename button, other actions such as adding a new field to the album are performed using the context menu of the album-summary-table

The album-summary-table allows (via context-menu) to reorder, delete and add fields. These actions are performed immediately.
