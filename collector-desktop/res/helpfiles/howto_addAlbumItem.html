How-to add a new Album Item

If you want to add a new album item, then simply click on the "Add new Album-Item" button at the bottom of the left sidebar. Alternatively, you can use the menu (Album-Items -> Add new Album-Item).

In both cases, the right sidebar will show the appropriate fields to add a new album item also known as record!

It is not required to fill out every field, this also holds for picture.
